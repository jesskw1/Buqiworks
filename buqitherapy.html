What is Buqi?

Buqi is a complete system of healing and self-healing with roots in
Chinese energy therapy going back over a thousand years, it is recorded
in Chinese history books as being a method by which some people could
heal others without touching.

Dr Shen discovered through his own practice and teachings of Tai
jiquan and Qigong, that these profound methods of Qi cultivation
could also be applied to help heal others, with particular emphasis on
postural correction and treating emotional imbalance. These discoveries
became the basis for the development of the modern Buqi system.

Buqi, as it is today, combines medical and healing knowledge from both
East and West aiming to treat the root causes of disease and thereby
release negative patterns, bringing the person back into balance and
enabling health to be restored naturally.

Buqi Treatment

During a Buqi treatment a patient may be asked to sit, stand or lie
down. After taking details of the patient’s health concerns and medical
history, the practitioner will diagnose and then begin to treat. All the techniques are designed to both heal the patient and protect the
practitioner from absorbing the patient’s Binqi.

Most techniques are therefore hands off and directing Binqi away from
both patient and therapist. A patient may experience a variety of
physical and emotional sensations such as the spinal column
lengthening, heat, itching, cold, the sensation of pain moving and
clearing, crying, laughing and often, afterwards, a sensation of deep
peace and relaxation.

In addition to treatment, a patient may be given specific exercises,
empowering them to continue to improve and maintain their own health.

easy it is to change the root cause of your health problem.
