TAIJIWUXGONG

What is Taijiwuxgong?

Taijiwuxigong is a Qigong and exercise system that has been researched
and refined by Dr.Shen for more than 40 years. Based on and developed
from the self-healing element of Tai Chi, it uses Daoyin exercises and
Spontaneous Movement to activate the Dantain, clean the meridian
system and expel binqi (sick information) from the body.

Employing a variety of moving, standing, sitting and lying down
exercises combined with breathing, sound and mental concentration
techniques, Taijiwuxigong focuses on activating the Dantain (the main energy centre of the body), correcting postural alignment and
awakening one’s sensitivity to natural spontaneour movements.

Exercises aim to integrate the body, breath, Yuan (vital) Qi and the mind.

My Classes

At the beginning, learning about the importance of posture is
key and this combined with specific exercises encourages the
release of emotional trauma and physical and mental blockages and tension. Gradually, this clearing and releasing of old
‘information’ helps to develop greater clarity of mind a stronger
sense of self-worth and internal peace and a deepening compassion
towards one self and others.

Taijiwuxigong has proved effective in treating and/or relieving
symptoms of a variety of chronic and debilitating health conditions
and can be adapted to suit all ages and states of health.
